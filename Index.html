Sometimes Melbourne: Reviews and writing about theatre

Independent theatre in Melbourne is glorious. As are all the companies, creatives, artists, producers and techies who make it. Indie theatre has always been supported by indie media – passionate writers and honest commentators whose lives are filled with theatre. Sometimes Melbourne has been celebrating, analysing and sharing news about indie theatre for over 15 years. And is still going.
