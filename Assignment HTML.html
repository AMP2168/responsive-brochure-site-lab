Sometimes Melbourne: Reviews and writing about theatre

Independent theatre in Melbourne is glorious. As are all the companies, creatives, artists, producers and techies who make it. Indie theatre has always been supported by indie media – writers and commentators whose lives are filled with theatre. Indie media celebrate, analyse and share.
